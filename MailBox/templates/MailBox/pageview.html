Dear Students,

There is a placement opportunity for the final year students of B.Tech (except Civil), M.Tech & P.hD. 

Details can be viewed here: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1Xd679daOIYFVhu4V-r8VXoq8d60ZflGy?usp=sharing

Interested students can apply here: https://forms.gle/ghUPBw1fC9de697v6
Please note after applying here, you need to apply on the TCS portal. Refer document in the google folder [mandatory].

Deadline: 10th October Midnight

Eligibility
  • Candidate should be 2021 passing out students only. No 2020 passing out student is allowed in this drive.
  • CGPA  of 8 and above and percentage of 80% or above in highest qualification (Grad or post Grad).
  • 60% or above 10th, 12th, diploma, graduation.
  • No pending backlogs at the time of selection process.
  • Steams considered are  Computer Science, ECE, EEE, Mathematics, Statistics, Bio Sciences / Biotechnology / Bioinformatics, Mechanical
  • Completion of all courses Class Xth onwards within the stipulated time as specified by your University/Institute and as per the TCSL Selection Guidelines without any extended education. For example, if you have a backlog in the final semester of BE, it is considered as extended education as the stipulated time to complete BE is 4 years.
  • It is mandatory to declare the gaps/arrears/backlogs, if any, during your academic and/or work experience. Break-in education should not be due to extended education. Any break in education/work experience should not exceed 24 months and is permissible only for valid reasons. Relevant document proof, as applicable, will be checked for gaps in education.
  • Only Full time courses will be considered (Part Time/Correspondence courses will not be considered).
  • You should be minimum of 18 years of age and a maximum of 28 years of age to be eligible to apply for the TCSL Selection process
  • Candidates who have applied to TCSL and have not been successful in clearing the TCSL selection process are not eligible to re-apply to TCSL within six months from the date on which they had attended such selection test and/or interview. You are not eligible to appear for the TCSL selection process within six months of the previous unsuccessful attempt.
Job Description

TCS Research and Innovation link is https://www.tcs.com/research-innovation.  
 
Also, students need to register on TCS Next step portal and fill the complete application form. (Help Documents are uploaded in google folder)

Important Note: No student will be allowed to sit for the interview process without the application form and complete educational documents. It is a mandatory step and needs to be followed strictly.
